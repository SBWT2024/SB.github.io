About Bianca & Shane

Their Story

Bianca and Shane first crossed paths at church, a place that would become the foundation of their beautiful journey together. Although they had spoken many times before, it wasn't until the perfect moment, guided by divine timing, that they truly noticed each other. Their first date marked the beginning of a love story that would blossom into something extraordinary.

Their relationship has been filled with memorable milestones, from their first date to their engagement. Together, they share a passion for adventure, enjoying activities like hiking, parkruns, and exploring new places. Whether they're embarking on exciting adventures or relaxing at home with a movie, their bond grows stronger with each passing day. They also cherish spending quality time with their families, which has further deepened their connection.

Bianca, a dedicated nurse, and Shane, who works in the information technology field, complement each other perfectly. Their love is a beautiful blend of care, support, and shared dreams. This special day marks the beginning of a new chapter in their lives, and they are thrilled to celebrate their love with all of you. The theme of their celebration is one of gratitude and joy, as they honor the journey that brought them together and look forward to a future filled with love and happiness.
